It's a hard G

I grew up in Istanbul, where my brother taught me everything he knew about computers and drawing comics. At the tender age of 13, I moved to Texas. After strolling leisurely through academia, I left for New York and started interning at digital agencies which sparked my interest in design. I taught myself the basics and began working as a graphic designer.

Surviving New York mostly unscathed, I came back to Austin, picked up some web design projects and began working for the most discerning name in travel. Still hungry, I decided to learn even more; currently studying full-stack development, and in need of a nap.
