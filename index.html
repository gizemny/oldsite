My name is Gizem

With a hard g

I grew up in Istanbul; At the tender age of 13, moved to Texas. After strolling leisurely through academia, I left for New York to work in publishing but found myself interning at digital agencies which sparked my interest in design. Shortly after, I began working as a graphic designer.

Surviving New York mostly unscathed, I came back to Austin, picked up a few new skills, some web design projects and a dog. I have been working as a web designer in a small team. Still hungry, I decided to go back to school to learn even more; currently studying full-stack development, and in need of a nap.

Andrew Harper Auctions

  • Launch: January 2016
  • Client: Andrew Harper
  • Category: Visual Design Lead

The old auction system, with it's cluttered and outdated design, wasn't attracting many users. My goal was to let these amazing auction deals breathe with an attractive design which would also allow the users to easily locate information and take action.

Since the redesign, we have seen significant increase in user engagement and auction sales.
