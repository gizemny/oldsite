My name is Gizem

Between us, it's a hard G

I grew up in Istanbul, and at the tender age of 13, moved to Texas. After strolling leisurely through academia, I left for New York to work in publishing but found myself interning at digital agencies which sparked my interest in design. Shortly after, I began working as a graphic designer.

Surviving New York mostly unscathed, I came back to Austin, picked up a few new skills, some web design projects and a dog. I have been working as a web designer in a small team. Still hungry, I decided to go back to school to learn even more; currently studying full-stack development, and in need of a nap.
